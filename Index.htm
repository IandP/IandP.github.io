Registering your account

Before you can register your account, access is granted to MeterVue by your administrator.

If you were emailed an activation link, follow the instructions in the email to get started.

If you have not received an activation link or it has expired, you can request a new activation link by clicking on the "Register Account" tab underneath the login box.

Fill in your details and an new activation link will be sent to your registered email address.

Forgotten Password

If you forget your password you can request a reset link to be emailed to your registered email address. Follow the instructions contained within the email to reset your password

Forgotten Login Email Address

If you can't remember which email address is required for access to MeterVue, contact your MeterVue administrator
